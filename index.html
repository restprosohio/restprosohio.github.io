On site, our IICRC-certified team leader and technicians will quickly assess your situation, stop the migration of water to unaffected areas of your home, extract the water, and remove water damaged materials. State of the art moisture detection meters and drying equipment are used to discover moisture intrusion and eliminate it from your home.

Water Damage Restoration Process

  1. We identify and stop the water source if not already done.
  2. We identify the type of water. The type of water will affect the restoration process used to restore your property.
  3. We survey the extent of water damage and inspect for safety issues.
  4. We move all furniture and/or property items as necessary.
  5. We extract and remove all standing water using pumps and extractors, and remove water-affected materials such as wet carpet, pad, drywall, trim, etc., as necessary.
  6. We use high speed air movers, commercial-grade dehumidifiers, and HEPA air filters to clean the air through the drying process in order to remove remaining bound moisture.
  7. We monitor the drying process from start to finish - in floors, walls, framing, drywall, wood, etc. - taking moisture level readings daily using state-of-the-art hygrometer and moisture sensor meters.
  8. We use sanitizing, anti-microbial, anti-bacterial, and/or deodorizing treatments as needed.
  9. To restore your property to its previous condition, we repair the damage and provide full reconstruction services, including:
    • Emergency services such as roof or plumbing repair
    • Drywall replacement, finishing, and texturing
    • Insulation replacement
    • Cabinet and fixture replacement
    • Painting
    • Hardwood, carpet, laminate, vinyl, and tile replacement
